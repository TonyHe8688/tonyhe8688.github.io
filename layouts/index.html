About This Lab

Tony He works on embedded Linux, Android single board computers, display integration, and industrial HMI systems. This site is a personal engineering archive for board bring-up notes, hardware module checks, display debugging, and production validation workflows.

The work is connected with practical embedded product development at Avontek, while the articles here stay focused on technical notes, checklists, and implementation details.
